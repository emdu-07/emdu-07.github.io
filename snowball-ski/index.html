Snowball Ski Race!

Instructions Pt. 1

You have 60 seconds to make your snowball as big as you can.

Use the arrow keys (or WASD) to steer. Press P to pause/resume.

Roll over snowpiles to collect points.

Ice makes you go faster (and points stop ticking).

Instructions Pt. 2

Smaller snowballs add score. Bigger ones splat you.

Avoid trees and rocks.

Snowmobiles can run you over.

Warning sign means a snowmobile is incoming.

Controls

Arrows or WASD to move. Press P to pause/resume.
